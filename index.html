Program Overview

The Executive Certification in Fintech & Open Banking, with Specializations in Digital Banking, Entrepreneurship and AIML & Financial Blockchain is a professional course comprising of live lectures and self paced content delivered by industry practitioners.

This program is for every BFSI professional aspiring to lead digital initiatives, as well as for CXOs & Board Members to guide organisations for strategic initiatives & innovations, as well as for Entrepreneurs to plan and build scalable business models.

It is also for Product, Business, Operations and Compliance professionals who intend to make the best use of technology to usher in the next wave of growth in their organizations. The purpose of this course is to give a well rounded understanding of the Business and Tech aspects so professionals who are in cross-functional roles or are pivoting there can seamlessly lead digital transformation. The Digital Fifth also offers placement assistance.

The sessions will be conducted every Sunday, from 3:00 - 7:00 PM over the course of 20 weeks. Refer the table below for the duration breakdown of each segment.

Why The Digital Fifth ?

Our Master Classes and Certification Programs are eminent in the BFSI industry. We have witnessed first-time entrepreneurs emerging from our alumni batches and have seen founders reform their business models after attending our sessions on Digital Lending, Open Banking, Digital Banking, Digital Payments, Embedded Finance and more. Professionals have also attended multiple programs to maximize their learnings.

The demands inherent to the digitisation of businesses are pressuring traditional players without exception. ‘Disrupt or Die’ is the new mantra, and today, many Banks and NBFCs are also entering the era of Digital Transformation. In order to implement this, there must be a drastic shift in the mindset of doing business and a new skill set must be adopted. Upon this transformation, various roles will be impacted globally. As per our estimation, 20,000+ digital professionals will be required in the financial industry in the next 2 years in India.

The Digital Fifth is focused on getting participants ‘industry ready’, and we do not believe in any theoretical jargon. The program is experience and case-study led, with the lead trainers consulting BFSI & Fintech entities. They bring their understanding from their assignments into the training program so participants have an on-the-ground perspective.
